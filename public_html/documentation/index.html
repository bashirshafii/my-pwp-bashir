About Me

Mr. Bashir Shafii, Founder and Chief Technology Officer, is a seasoned technology professional who has helped Fortune 500 companies implement major technology projects. He has consulted for clients such as CIGNA, Merrill, Cargill, AmeriPrise, UCare, Prime Therapeutics and Optum. He holds the following certifications: IIBA Certified Business Analysis Professional, ITIL V3 Foundation, Microsoft SQL Server and Scrum Master Certification from the ScrumAlliance. Mr. Shafii earned a Bachelor of Science in Psychology from the University of North Dakota at Grand Forks, ND. Black Swan Solutions is an IT training and consulting firm. We help clients build robust web applications, and provide training and consulting services. We build and deploy applications for various platforms such as Microsoft and Open Source technologies.
